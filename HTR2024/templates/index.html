Welcome to PulsePort

Problem

People overdose on prescription medication, leading to severe health risks and fatalities.

Solution

PulsePort is an innovative system designed to help users manage their medication and stay connected with healthcare professionals.

Key Features

  • Admin-User System: Healthcare professionals act as Admins, guiding users through their health journey.
  • User Reports: Users can report critical details like medication use, vitals, and emergency contacts to their Admins.
  • Appointment Scheduling: Users can easily book drop-in meetings with their Admins for personalized support.
